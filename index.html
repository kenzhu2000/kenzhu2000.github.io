Research

My work has been focused on enabling machines to understand natural languages and further generate these languages. This is one of the most challenging tasks in artificial intelligence. My recent focus has been making NLP models smaller, more accessible and interpretable, and developing interdisciplinary NLP techniques, such as animal language processing and NLP for mental health.

I'm leading the Advanced Data And Programming Technology Lab (ADAPT-Lab) .
