Research

My past work was focused on enabling machines to understand natural languages and further generate these languages, which is one of the most challenging tasks in artificial intelligence. My recent focus has been more interdisciplinary. At the heart of my research, I'm trying to answer the big science question: what's the relationship between intelligence and language? Specifically, i) is language (skill) a necessary part (or outcome) of intelligence? ii) can the use of a language or the lack of which affect the development of intelligence? To answer these questions, I have embarked on the following projects.
